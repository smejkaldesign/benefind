## 4. How It Works

### Headline Options

1. **Three Steps. Five Minutes. Real Answers.**
2. **Simple as 1, 2, 3**

### Step 1: Answer Simple Questions

We ask about your household, income, and situation. No jargon, no confusing forms. Just plain questions in plain language.

### Step 2: Get Matched Instantly

Our AI compares your answers against eligibility rules for 12+ programs. You'll see your results in seconds, not weeks.

### Step 3: See Your Next Steps

For every program you may qualify for, we show you what documents you need, where to apply, and what to expect.
